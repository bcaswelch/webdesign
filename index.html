Features

Supports standard desktop parts to make your life easier and more cost efficient when it comes to upgrading parts

ViPC is built around a high quality aluminium case, CNC cut with venting holes and brackets to ensure the outside remains sleek while keeping the inside protected
Our custom fans were designed specifically for ViPC, Using the maximum amount of space possible to decrease fan noise and low system temperatures
