Meet ViPC, a revolutionary computer platform that combines the power of a full-sized desktop with the portability of a mini computer

Modular

ViPC is a family of products that all work seamlessly together, providing you with a worry-free experience.

Small

ViPC is a new standard built for the m-ITX form-factor, set to change the way small form factor computer has been viewed in the past.

Powerful

Unlike many small form factor computers, ViPC uses standard desktop components, giving the user more power in a smaller package.

Silent

By giving each component its own cooling chamber, ViPC is able to keep low temperatures while also minimizing fan speed.

Use Cases

ViPC is designed for a wide audience, being flexible enough to place itself in nearly any situation.

Designers

With support for up to 32Gb of system memory and the option to house workstation graphics, artists working with 3D modeling or rendering can have a small yet powerful system to keep up with their work. If working in a studio, ViPC is easy to move around and can be kept off the table to maintain a clean workplace.

Gamers

Enjoy full desktop performance in games, playing at the highest resolutions and fastest framerates. Going to a LAN party? Gone are the days of lugging around large desktop cases. Simply put ViPC in your backpack and you're good to go.

Photographers

Hard drive speed is key for photographers, so we incorporated the option for RAID 0 (using two+ drives to double disk speed) to give users who need it fast and dependable disk speed. ViPC can also be mounted to any VESA (mounting standard) compatible monitor, giving you the freedom to choose the type you screen you desire.
